What 4x4 Response Wants From You

  • A desire to help your Community
  • Good sense of humour
  • Tact and Diplomacy
  • A flashing orange lamp
  • Towing equipment i.e. Recovery Strap, etc.
  • Be responsible for your own Road Tax , MOT and Insurance (Proof required)
  • Maintain your vehicle to a road-worthy standard
  • Be on WhatsApp
  • Knowledge of What3Words
  • Joining cost is £25 for the first year. Annual renewal is £15 p.a.
