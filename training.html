Training

As a member of Herts 4x4 Response Group we aim to provide you with training and skills to enable you to respond correctly to most situations that may arise.

Training opportunities are organised with the intention of raising your confidence levels to handle your 4x4 vehicle over uneven and rough terrain.

Use of some specialised equipment and handling situations, you may come across, in adverse conditions and inclement weather.
